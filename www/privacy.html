Data Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our priority. This document explains how GECWC Smart Entry collects, uses, and protects your information to provide a secure campus environment.

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide digital gate passes and maintain campus security, the Application collects basic profile information provided during registration. This includes your Full Name, Roll Number, College Email Address, Mobile Number, Branch, Room Number, and Profile Picture. We also generate a secure, anonymous hardware ID to lock your account to your specific physical device to prevent pass sharing.

2. Location Data (Geofencing)

The Application requires access to your device's GPS location exclusively for proximity verification (Geofencing).

  • Location data is strictly used to verify that you are within the allowed radius of the college gate.
  • We do not track your location continuously in the background.
  • We do not save your GPS coordinates to our database.

3. Camera & Storage Access

The Application requests Camera and/or Photo Library permissions strictly for setting up your user profile picture (for visual identification by guards) and for scanning entry QR codes if necessary. We do not access, view, or upload any other photos from your device.

4. Data Security & Storage

Your data is transmitted securely and stored in an official, encrypted Google Apps Script / Google Workspace environment designated by the administration. Entry and Exit logs are accessible exclusively by authorized college administrators and security personnel for campus management and safety purposes. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

5. Contact Developer

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, app permissions, or data deletion requests, please contact the primary developer directly.
