Data Privacy & Security

We believe in complete transparency. This document outlines exactly how your data is used to secure the GECWC campus.

1. Information Collected

We collect basic profile information provided during registration: Name, Roll Number, Email, Mobile, Branch, Room, and Profile Picture. We also generate an anonymous hardware ID linked to your physical device to prevent account sharing.

2. Location Data

GPS access is required exclusively to verify you are standing near the college gate (Geofencing).

  • We DO NOT track you in the background.
  • We DO NOT save your coordinates.

3. Camera Access

Camera permissions are strictly for taking your profile picture (so guards can identify you) and scanning official QR codes. We cannot view or access your personal photo gallery.

4. Data Security

Your data is encrypted and stored in an official Google Workspace database. Entry logs are accessible only by authorized college security personnel. We do not sell or share data with third parties.
